Our Tools and Resources

We have curated the best list of tools and resources across the internet (again, only to best of our knowledge) , some made by us , others 3rd party.

  1. LeetCode: This is the holy grail for cracking coding rounds of best companies, as almost all questions are directly from here! One of the most important and best website to practice DSA and master it! People have made it to dream companies by supporting their rigourous studies with leetcode practice! Grind Leetcode!
  2. Geeks For Geeks: This site is a world of it own! Anything you stuck on? Gfg likely has an article on it! They have the best articles and many practice problems to help students! Articles are really clear and helps understanding hard topics in best way possible! Must explore site!
  3. Hacker Rank:HackerRank is one of the best platform to begin your coding journey. Makes it easy to understand basics of coding and some topics like OOPS.
  4. Hacker Earth:It is same as Hacker rank. It helps build concepts of OOPS and other tricky topics.
  5. Codechef:Code-Chef is an online educational program and competitive programming community of global programmers. Code-Chef started as an educational initiative in 2009 by Directi, an Indian software company. In 2020, it became owned by Unacademy.
  6. Codeforces:It is also a competitive programming platform and it can be a bit harder to solve questions on this platform in comparison to codechef. Move to codeforces after atleast achieving three star in codechef.
  7. Practice Ladder(CF):Codeforces questions sorted according to rating and progress is easily trackable.
  8. CodeForces Analyzer!:A tool built by us to provide you with better analysis of your performance on codeforces.
